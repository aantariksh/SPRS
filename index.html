About Us

SPRS And Co. LLP, Company Secretaries (SPRS) is a firm of Company Secretaries and Governance Professionals. The firm derives its name from its founding Partners, CS (DR.) Sanjay P. Parab and CS Rahul P. Sahasrabuddhe

The spectrum of services provided by SPRS encompass setting up of business entities,advisory and consultancy services in Corporate Laws, Securities Laws and other business laws. We at SPRS ensure all the statutory and regulatory compliances of our clients, truly a One Stop Compliance Solution. Through our network of professionals across India, we are able to provide wide spectrum of solutions at various locations in India.
