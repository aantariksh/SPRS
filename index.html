About Us

SPRS And Co. LLP is a firm of qualified Company Secretaries and Governance Professionals. The firm derives its name from the initials of its founding partners, CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab and CS Rahul Sahasrabuddhe.

The spectrum of services provided by us encompasses setting up of business entities, advisory and consultancy services in Corporate Laws, Securities Laws and other Business Laws. We at SPRS ensure all the statutory and regulatory compliances of our clients.

The array of services provided by us also include drafting, appearances and pleadings before the various statutory and quasi judicial bodies like National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT), Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT), Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and Consumer Commissions.
