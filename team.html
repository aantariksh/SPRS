Our Team

Our Team

CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab

CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab, founding partner of SPRS And Co. LLP is a Fellow Member of ICSI, Post Graduate in Law and Commerce and has over 20 years of experience in the field of Secretarial and Legal functions.

CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab is a Doctor in Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Business Practices from Savitribai Phule Pune University in a subject of Corporate Governance.

Before commencing his practise, CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab has worked with the reputed companies listed with the BSE Limited and National Stock Exchange of India Limited.

CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab is a visiting faculty at various reputed colleges and has written more than 10 research papers and participated in more than 23 State, National and International Conferences / workshops and Professional Development Programs.

CS (Dr) Sanjay Parab is instrumental and vastly experienced in corporate advisory services, secretarial audits and due diligences.

CS Rahul Sahasrabuddhe

CS Rahul Sahasrabuddhe, founding partner of SPRS And Co. LLP, is a Fellow Member of ICSI and Post Graduate in Commerce. He has worked with NSDL, NSE, B & K Securities, Emkay Global and CLSA. He is specialized in Corporate and Securities Laws litigation matters.

He was one of the Two “Professional Expert Members” on the Board of Studies (BOS), St. Xavier’s College, University of Mumbai.

He has authored columns in newspapers and magazines on varied subjects including Law and Finance.

He is regular speaker at various programs conducted by Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI), Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI) and St. Xaviers’ Management Institute.

He has held several positions at Chapter level and was the Chairman of Western India Region of Institute of Company Secretaries of India (WIRC of ICSI) for the year 2020.

CS Ninad Sahasrabuddhe

CS Ninad Sahasrabuddhe, founding partner of SAHASRABUDDHE PARAB & CO. LLP, a sister firm of SPRS and Co. LLP is an Associate Member of ICSI, Graduate in Law and Post Graduate in Commerce.

CS Ninad Sahasrabuddhe is associated with the SPRS And Co. LLP for more than five years and is vastly experienced in handling of listed companies compliances, audits under the Companies Act, 2013 & other SEBI Laws.

CS Ninad Sahasrabuddhe handles corporate litigation matters including mergers, demergers & amalgamations, cases related to Oppression and Mismanagement, litigation under Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 etc.

CS Ninad Sahasrabuddhe is involved as a faculty to various trainings and professional development programs organised by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India.

CS Rita Virji Saparia

CS Rita Sapariya, senior associate is an Associate Member of the ICSI, Post Graduate in Commerce, having over 5 years of experience in the field of secretarial, audits and advisory services.

SPRS And Co. LLP

SPRS And Co. LLP, Company Secretaries (SPRSCS), is a firm of Company Secretaries and Governance Professionals. The firm derives its name from its founding partners, CS Sanjay Parab and CS Rahul Sahasrabuddhe.
