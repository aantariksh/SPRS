Incorporation and Formation

  • Incorporation of Companies under the Companies Act, 2013
  • Incorporation of Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs) under the LLP Act, 2008

Secretarial Services

  • Event based compliances including Annual Filing and XBRL filing.
  • Periodical compliances as per the Companies Act, 2013 and SEBI Regulations.
  • Conversion of LLPs into Companies.
  • Conversion of Private Limited Company to Public Limited Company and vice versa.
  • Retainership Services.
  • Striking off and winding up of Companies.

Audits and Certifications

  • Secretarial Audit under Companies Act, 2013
  • Intermediaries Audits as per the SEBI Laws and Regulations
  • Corporate Governance Reports and Certifications
  • Legal due diligence
  • Secretarial Compliance Certifications

Litigations and Representations

  • Drafting, appearances and pleadings before the NCLT, NCLAT, SEBI, SAT, RBI, Consumer Commissions
  • Litigation under the Companies Act, 2013 Litigation under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016
  • Oppression and Mismanagement.

Transactional Services

  • Mergers, Demergers and Amalgamations
  • Takeovers and acquisitions
  • Reduction of share capital
  • Public and Private placement of securities
  • Listing and de-listing of securities

Advisory Services

  • Opinions on critical issues in corporate and securities laws

FEMA Compliances

  • Ad hoc compliances under the FEMA
  • 'Due Diligence' Certification in Multi Banking/ Consortium banking finance matters.
  • Compounding under FEMA
  • FEMA Audits
  • FIPB Approvals

NBFCs

  • Advisory on Registration
  • Routine and event based compliances
  • NBFC Audit

Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Registration of Trusts, Societies and NGOs under CSR
  • Advisory on fund allocation
